Bouncy Golf

  • Originality: This game is a fun version of golf, the player has to try to hit the ball into the hole at the corner.
  • Technical Merit: It uses physics and sounds.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I got even more familiar with phaser (have used it before) and I got to refresh certain concepts. Making and using assets was a bit tricky.
  • Prototype Assets: I made certain assests from scratch and some from phaser examples and other places.
    • Background image: Background
    • Player: Player
    • Ball: Ball
    • Sound: Sound
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I did not borrow the idead from anywhere, it was just a game I built as I kept adding assests keeping a golfer theme in mind. As far as borrowed code goes I either looked up documentation or used the phaser examples for certain small things (like how to add sound).
  • Phaser Examples: Phaser Examples
  • High Concept: The overall goal is to get the ball in the hole (just like golf).
  • Theme: Golfer.
  • Mandated Variety: Not Sure
  • Prototype Goal: The overall prototype goal is to test physics and sounds in phaser.
  • Player Experience Goals: Just have fun.
  • Gameplay: Use the left and right arrow keys to move the player around, the up arrow key to jump. Try to hit the ball as it bounces into the hole.
  • Strategies: Do not jump on the ball oftened as this decreased the ball's bounce.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Not applicable for this prototype
  • Target Audience: Everyone who likes to play really simple games to pass time.
  • Play Time: It can take upto 5 to 10 mins for getting the ball into the hole once. Maybe less if the player is lucky or really good at it.
